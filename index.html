How to Use

  1. Modify the values in the GAME_CONFIG object on the left
  2. Click "Run Code" to see your changes instantly
  3. Try changing:
    • playerSpeed - How fast your ship moves (try 20!)
    • playerColor - Your ship's color (try '#ff00ff' for pink!)
    • playerSize - How big your ship is (try 80!)
    • enemySpeed - How fast enemies fall (try 0.5 for slow motion!)
    • scoreMultiplier - Points per enemy (try 100!)
  4. Use arrow keys or WASD to move, Space to shoot
  5. Click "Quick Challenges" for preset modifications
